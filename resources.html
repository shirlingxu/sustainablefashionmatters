Did you know?

The manufacturing process for a single top wastes over 2,700 liters of water. Water is a valuable but scarce resource that we shouldn't take for granted. This mass consumption of water isn't good for the environment, and it can potentially increase the cost of fashion in years to come.

Additionally, fabric costs a lot of energy to produce, which is why it has a large carbon footprint. The textile industry is one of the largest sources of greenhouse gas emissions in the world because of the manufacturing process of the fabric and the transportation process to the consumer.
